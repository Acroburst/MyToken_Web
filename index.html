WHAT IS MYTOKEN?
THE DIGITAL
FUTURE
MyToken is an open source project made to be a decentralised software-based currency for all users. It is initially created to be a basic ERC-20 smart contract token for learning purposes and act as an applicable template for integration uses. It acts as a simple cryptocurrency that allows transactions of tokens between accounts, giving individuals more free, convenient & secured interactions in the community of the cyberspace economy.
